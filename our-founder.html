OUR FOUNDER

OUR FOUNDER

Oyeleke Oluwafolakanmi Founder/CEO- Is an agriculture and energy enthusiast, he has over 15 years of experience in several industries including construction, agriculture, energy, real estate, FMCG, sales, and tech. He is a passionate, driven, and determined individual. An astute businessman, He is the founder of Feed Afrique and B.S.A.C.AG. Limited.

An alumnus of Orange Corners Nigeria, an initiative of the Dutch government and the Soft Landing New York program by the Koffmann Southern tier incubator in partnership with Binghamton and Cornell university. He sits on the board of his family companies. He firmly believes agriculture is the surest path to alleviate poverty and spread the wealth amongst young people in Africa. He is also pursuing a Master's degree in Architecture at the University.

About Us

B.S.A.C.AG. a subsidiary of Feed Afrique is a social agribusiness transforming the lives of small holder/rural farmers and their communities though agriculture.
