ABOUT US

B.S.A.C.AG. a subsidiary of Feed Afrique is a social agribusiness transforming the lives of small holder/rural farmers and their communities though agriculture, by building farm estates to produce crops and livestock and process into finished goods, while also training and empowering the local populace.

OUR MISSION

We are on a mission to transform and spur growth on the African continent through agriculture by producing crops and livestock and processing them into finished goods. Most of the agricultural waste in Africa happens during production, so we must add value to these products and store them to give them longer shelf life and be more useful.

OUR VISION

To Spearhead the agricultural revolution on the African continent, by moving from substinence to mechanized agriculture where we will develop large farm estates that will empower the local populace and in turn feed the continent.
